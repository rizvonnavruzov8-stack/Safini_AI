# Safini AI - Demo Video Guide 🎥

To impress your IT Director, the demo video should be concise (90-120 seconds) and focus on the **Core Value Prop**: *Earning screen time through productive habits.*

## 🎙️ Should you explain?
**Yes!** A "Voiceover" makes it much more professional. Speak clearly and explain *why* a feature matters, not just what it is.

## 📝 The Script (Demo Flow)

1.  **Introduction (10s)**: 
    *   *Action*: Show the Kids Dashboard. 
    *   *Script*: "Hi [Director Name], here is the Safini AI MVP. It's a platform where kids earn 'Time Coins' for productive tasks."
2.  **Kid's Mode - Earning (30s)**:
    *   *Action*: Tap on a task (e.g., Logic Puzzle or Steps) and show the "Proof Submission" UI.
    *   *Script*: "When a child completes a task, they submit proof. This gamifies their daily learning and exercise habits."
3.  **Parent's Mode - Approval (30s)**:
    *   *Action*: Switch to the Parent Dashboard. Show the Approval Queue and approve a task.
    *   *Script*: "The parent gets a notification, reviews the proof, and approves it. This builds trust and verification into the system."
4.  **Kids' Mode - Spending (20s)**:
    *   *Action*: Go to the Reward Shop and unlock a session.
    *   *Script*: "Finally, those earned coins are spent to unlock entertainment apps like YouTube or Roblox, turning screen time into a reward for hard work."
5.  **Conclusion (10s)**:
    *   *Action*: Show the Insights/Dashboard summary.
    *   *Script*: "The MVP currently handles the full loop with local storage. Phase 2 will add sync and device enforcement."

## 🛠️ How to record?

### Option A: Mobile Phone (Real Device)
*   **Android**: Use the built-in Screen Recorder (usually in the quick settings/swipe down menu).
*   **iOS**: Use the built-in Screen Recording feature.
*   *Tip*: Turn on "Show Taps" in Developer Options (Android) so the director can see where you are clicking.

### Option B: On your Computer (Emulator)
*   Use software like **OBS Studio** or **QuickTime** (Mac) to record the emulator window.
*   This often looks "cleaner" because there are no finger reflections.
